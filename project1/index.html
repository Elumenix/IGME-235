Welcome To Chiaroscuro

Chiaroscuro is a high fantasy world that is controlled and populated by supernatural art and artists. Artist guilds have risen up seeking to master the supernatural power of this art in order to get ahead in society. Due to the nature of Art and power it has in this world has made the culture of Chiaroscuro very hedonistic, superficial, competitive, as well as pleasurable. Player will navigate this world with one goal: To Become The Artista.
